Avatar

Avatar showcases the user's picture for the login profile, for the navigation bar and also for other user information tabs.

Alerts

Alerts are used to attract user's attention for important information without interrupting the user's flow.

Alerts are available in 4 types- Primary alert, Success alert, Error alert, Warning alert and in two different styles: Solid Alert and Outline Alert.
